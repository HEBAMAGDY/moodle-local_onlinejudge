Test Cases

Every testcase will be sent to the submissions and judged seperately.

Grade: The grade for one testcase. If pass the test, student will get the grade. Otherwize, get zero. Sum of grades of all the testcases is the final grade.

Input: The input data which will be sent to the stdin of submissions.

Output: The output data which will be compared with the data from submissions' stdout to judge.

Feedback: The message will be showed to the students who didn't pass the testcase. Useful if you want to give the student some hints or instructions.
